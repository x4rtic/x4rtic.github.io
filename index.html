Currently Working On

Bushido: Fallen Era

Bushido: Fallen Era is an independent MMORPG set in a dark fantasy world inspired by feudal Japan. The game blends traditional role-playing mechanics with deep lore, faction-based warfare, and strategic combat.

  • ⚔️ Classless progression system – develop your playstyle freely
  • 🌐 Persistent open world with dynamic events and evolving story arcs
  • 🗡️ Real-time skill-based combat — dodge, parry, and strike
  • 🏯 Player-run factions with territory control and reputation systems
  • 🎨 Custom pixel-art style with handcrafted environments

Development is in early stages. I'm building core systems from the ground up including networking, world simulation, and server infrastructure.
